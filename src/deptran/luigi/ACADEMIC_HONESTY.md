## Academic Use & Attribution Requirements

If you use this repository or **substantial portions of it** in any of the following contexts:

- University coursework  
- Graded assignments  
- Course projects  
- Take-home exams  
- Academic submissions or reports

you **must**:

1. Clearly attribute the work to:  
   - **Avjot Singh, Kunal Chadha, Gautam Sardana, and Angad Singh**  
   - The upstream **Mako** project

2. Explicitly state that the work was derived from or inspired by this repository

3. Comply with the academic integrity policies of your institution

Failing to do so may constitute academic misconduct under institutional policies.

---

## Prohibited Misrepresentation

The following are considered misrepresentation in academic or professional contexts:

- Claiming this repository or its results as entirely original coursework  
- Presenting the Luigi protocol or evaluation as independently developed work  
- Omitting attribution in resumes, project reports, or academic submissions

---

## Professional & Resume Use

You may reference this repository:

- As prior art, inspiration, or related work  
- As an example of an open-source system you studied or extended

You **must not**:

- Claim sole authorship of this work if you were not a contributor  
- List this repository as your own course project if you were not an author

---

## Enforcement & Responsibility

Academic integrity is enforced by educational institutions, not by software licenses.

Responsibility for ethical use lies with the user of the code, not with the original authors.

This notice exists to:

- Provide clear expectations  
- Prevent accidental misuse  
- Preserve academic integrity for all parties
